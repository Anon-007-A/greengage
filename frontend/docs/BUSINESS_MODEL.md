# GreenGauge Business Model

## Executive Summary

GreenGauge is a NextGen Loan Market Analytics Platform that combines AI-powered covenant breach prediction, comprehensive ESG analytics, and automated regulatory compliance reporting. The platform addresses critical pain points for banks managing syndicated loan portfolios, particularly in the growing green financing sector.

**Value Proposition:**
- **Prevent Covenant Breaches**: AI predicts breaches 90 days in advance with 85%+ accuracy, saving €5-15M per prevented breach
- **Automate Compliance**: CSRD, EU Taxonomy, and TCFD reporting automated, saving 120+ hours/year per bank
- **Green Financing Leadership**: Only solution combining covenant monitoring with green/ESG analytics

## Market Opportunity

  - CSRD report generation (monthly)
  - Email support

### Tier 2: Professional
- **Price**: €5,000/month (€60,000/year)

### Tier 3: Enterprise

2. **Regulatory Changes**: CSRD/EU Taxonomy rules may evolve
3. **Competition**: Large players (Bloomberg, Refinitiv) may build similar features
4. **Data Quality**: ML model accuracy depends on quality loan data

## Mitigation Strategies

2. **Regulatory Expertise**: Hire compliance experts to stay ahead of changes
3. **Product Differentiation**: Focus on green financing niche
4. **Data Partnerships**: Partner with data providers for quality inputs
