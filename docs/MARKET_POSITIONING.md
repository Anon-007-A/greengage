# GreenGauge: Market Positioning & Competitive Analysis

**Prepared for**: LMA EDGE Hackathon 2025  
**Date**: January 2025  
**Target Market**: Green Loan Market (€500B+ AUM)

---

## Executive Summary

GreenGauge positions itself as the **first unified covenant monitoring platform specifically for green loans**, addressing a critical market gap where legacy solutions (Bloomberg, Reuters, internal systems) lack integration, standardization, and ESG-specific analytics.

### Key Value Propositions

1. **Covenant Automation**: Real-time breach monitoring (vs. manual quarterly tracking)
2. **ESG-Integrated Covenants**: Sustainability-linked loan analytics (vs. financial-only)
3. **Interoperability**: Works with Bloomberg, LSEG, SWIFT, bank systems (vs. silos)
4. **Regulatory Ready**: CSRD, EU Taxonomy, TCFD compliance built-in (vs. custom bolt-ons)
